New Transaction

How this affects your dashboard

  • Expense → raises “Total expenses”, lowers “Balance (this month)”, lowers the chosen account balance, and increases that category chip.
  • Income → raises “Total income”, raises “Balance (this month)”, and raises the chosen account balance.
  • Starting balances don’t change monthly totals.
